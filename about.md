# About This Site

## Its Purpose

This site serves as a kind of interactive repository for a weak subset of things I found appropriate to place here. I decided to make this repository public for the small group of people I have met online, to provide an option to view such content. Posts will likely vary in substance and frequency.

This site also functions somewhat as an reactionary conduit to deal with the nature of modernity, and the isolation which comes as a consequence to anyone who sees this reality for what it is.

## Its Author

I attach this content to one of my online pseudonyms, *aufvektor*, to stall easy attachment with my professional persona. This is a considered position since employers are forced to restrict themselves to hiring only 'politically correct' persons, lest they be persecuted by primarily Jewish finance. Such an artifical selectivity is made possible by market conditions oversaturated with third world labour and purposefully constructed economic inefficiency.

There is no hope in creating a perfect facade and hiding anonymously forever, since one day man and even woman will have no choice but to be forced to make his stance known.

## On the Lack of Explaination

Many posts will lack background information and context, since I don't spend the time to explain them as a conseqeunce of the natural expectation that few will even do so much as read this text. Readers may choose to contact me for further explaination or background information if desired, since afterall, I owe it.
