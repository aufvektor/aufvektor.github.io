# Colophon

The site was developed across a few days by instructing Claude on the appearances desired. After various iterations, I choose the design on the basis of the grand medieval tradition - a time in which mankind was higher in every way other than technologically and scientifically - that is, higher in every way <i>that matters</i>.

Once there was a time in which I thought art and literature were useless, as a conseqeunce of exposure only to modern garbage. It is often only when we see the traditions of European civilisation that we begin to understand why such things matter - truely, it's importance is precisely why such an effort has been made to hide it all, and to stomp it out, else the broad masses would come to understand that which it pointed towards. The style of this website is intended to provide some of that exposure which the enemy has cunningly near-erradicted from our civilisation.

What else should a colophon read by near none say?
