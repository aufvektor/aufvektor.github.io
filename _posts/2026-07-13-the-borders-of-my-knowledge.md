It took me two decades plus or minus to figure out things which should essentially be obvious. Today I realise the stupidity and limitedness of my own mind, or rather, that of all men. I have thus found myself to only be capable of understanding things stripped down to only barebones basics and black and white certainities. This is why the only scientific fields that have been succesful are mathematics and science - what more has man actually discovered that is useful?

Yet these things are of the smallest concern. In the areas that matter, I have found nowhere in the span of modernity a method or a system of logical deduction by which things can be understood in a manner analogous to what the scientific method is to science. I am in a position where I am only sufficently capable of realising for myself basic truths that matter, yet I am completely stranded in figuring out what comes next. All I have is an instinct that this isn't home, that this isn't normal, even if everyone says that it is. I don't even know where it is that I am.

The Catholic Church (or at least, the tradition it inherited) stands as the only instituion by which I can sensibily intepret reality. There is only one other worldview where I feel that I am not the only one who sees the behind the mask of how things are.

Upon reflection, I have come to observe that my ideological breakthroughs were generally preceded by a question or a set of questions to which the supposed answered offered didn't compile, or questions which I had unanswered for long periods of time (this should be so obvious, yet it took me so long!) z. Motivated by this pattern, today I list questions which I still do not yet understand, and I believe that upon answering these questions, tommorow's obvious truths will be revealed. These are my milleinium prize problems, beyond that they are the questions of my twenty second year.

 - Why is it that the Catholic Church is perfectly correct on all of morality?
 - Under what circumstances must I obey the Vicar of Christ and his suboridinates, in lieu of the degeneration of the Catholic Church? (Or, dare I ask, is the seat vacant?)
 - Are the excommunications of the Society of Saint Pius X valid?
 - What actions am I supposed to take with respect to the ongoing mass extinction event of the European race?
 - What am I supposed to do in general? (Perhaps I know the answer to this one already - I am only given the minimal light that I require. I am not supposed to understand the big picture.)
 - Under what circumstances I am obliged to instruct others? (It never almost never works anyways)
 - Is it probable to have friends without maintaining a persona?
 - Is it probable to ever find a woman suitable for marriage? (This isn't even a question that I should state.)

And lastly, the question which reveals the complete lack of desire for truth that I have demonstrated:
 - Can the contradictions present in the later transcriptions of Bible, be solved by the Vulgate, thus invaliding my the reasoning which once caused the apostasy of a thirteen year old boy (as per the Council of Trent's declaration as to its validity, whereas no such guarentee exists for the modernist versions I thought were valid merely because they were supposedly catholic editions)?
