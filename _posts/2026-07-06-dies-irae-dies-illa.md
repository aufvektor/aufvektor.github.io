pages:
  - image: /assets/images/dies_irae/1.jpeg
    text: |
      The day of wrath, that day<br>
      will dissolve the world in ashes,<br>
      as David and the Sibyl bear witness.

      How great the trembling will be,<br>
      when the Judge shall come<br>
      to examine all things strictly!

      The trumpet, scattering a wondrous sound<br>
      through the tombs of every land,<br>
      will gather all before the throne.
  - image: /assets/images/dies_irae/2.jpeg
    text: |
      Death and nature will stand amazed,<br>
      when the creature rises again<br>
      to answer the One who judges.

      The written book will be brought forth,<br>
      in which all is contained,<br>
      whence the world shall be judged.

      When therefore the Judge is seated,<br>
      whatever lies hidden will appear:<br>
      nothing will remain unavenged.
  - image: /assets/images/dies_irae/3.jpeg
    text: |
      What then shall I, wretched man, say?<br>
      What patron shall I entreat,<br>
      when scarcely the just man is secure?

      King of fearful majesty,<br>
      who freely savest those to be saved,<br>
      save me, fount of mercy.

      Remember, merciful Jesus,<br>
      that I am the cause of Thy way:<br>
      lest Thou lose me on that day.
  - image: /assets/images/dies_irae/4.jpeg
    text: |
      Seeking me, Thou didst sit down weary;<br>
      Thou didst redeem me by suffering the Cross:<br>
      let not such labour be in vain.

      Just Judge of vengeance,<br>
      grant the gift of remission<br>
      before the day of reckoning.

      I groan, as one guilty;<br>
      my face reddens with guilt:<br>
      spare the supplicant, O God.
  - image: /assets/images/dies_irae/5.jpeg
    text: |
      Thou who didst absolve Mary,<br>
      and didst hear the thief,<br>
      hast given hope to me also.

      My prayers are not worthy,<br>
      but do Thou, who art good, deal kindly,<br>
      lest I burn in the everlasting fire.

      Grant me a place among the sheep,<br>
      and set me apart from the goats,<br>
      placing me at Thy right hand.
  - image: /assets/images/dies_irae/6.jpeg
    text: |
      When the accursed are confounded,<br>
      consigned to the bitter flames,<br>
      call me with the blessed.

      I pray, suppliant and bowed down,<br>
      my heart contrite as ashes:<br>
      take Thou care of my end.

      Tearful will be that day,<br>
      on which from the ashes shall rise again<br>
      the guilty man, to be judged.
  - image: /assets/images/dies_irae/7.jpeg
    text: |
      Spare him therefore, O God:<br>
      merciful Lord Jesus,<br>
      grant them rest. Amen.
---
